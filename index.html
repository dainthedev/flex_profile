Introduction

Hello, I am Dain Van Epps, an aspiring software and app developer. This is my portfolio. You will find it is bursting with innovative content sure to thrill and delight.

I live with my family in lovely Syracuse New York.

I have been studying IT for a few years now, but my experience and beackground goes well beyond that. I have worked and studied in fields ranging from environmental science to manufacturing to fire figthing, but I intend to make a career out of software. I am certain you will find my work exceeds your needs. I look forward to creating together.

About Me

I love the infinite possibilities of apps, programming and web design. I love that with minimal resources, anyone can make something that has an impact. That is what has drawn me to this field. I want to make things that matter and do so with people who care equally about producing valuable content.

Our world is simulataneously bigger and smaller. Our technological prowess makes the traversal of formerly unreachable destinations a possiblilty expanding our reach from the oceans depths to the stars. The vastness of the universe shrinks with every new accomplishment, but it is these very achivements that make the our universe all the richer. The swaths of tools and consumables at our disposal is breathtaking in the same way that the vastness of the uncharted seas was for our ancestors. There are some who say there are no more frontiers, but I say the greatest frontier has always been our own imagination and that is without limit. This is what I want to be a part of.
